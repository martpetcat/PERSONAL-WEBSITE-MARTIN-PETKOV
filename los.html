Branding Project
Martin Petkov

LO 1: Interactive Media Design

In this learning outcome you orient in the relevant tech, media and design landscape and create interactive media products that you have tested with users and stakeholders. You show how you tested your products, what your inspiration is and other reasons why you made your designs the way that they are.

LO 2: Documentation & Version Control

Managing and organizing project files, tracking progress, and ensuring structured documentation.

LO 3: Professional Design Practices

Understanding the iterative process and refining designs based on feedback.

LO 4: Research & Professional Standards

Conducting industry-standard research and implementing best practices in branding.

LO 5: Communication & Presentation

Enhancing project presentations and communicating design decisions effectively.

LO 6: Professional Identity

Building a strong personal and professional brand identity through research and experimentation.
